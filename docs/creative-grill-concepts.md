# Creative grill / swipe / refine concepts

Playful product ideas for refining plans with weird questions — not another AI PM chatbot.

**Spine:** Matt Pocock `/grill-me` (one question at a time, recommended answers, walk the tree) wearing a leather jacket.

## 10 original concepts

### 1. CHAR — 🔥 coolness 9 · hackathon YES
**Vibe:** Dating-app energy for your half-baked startup. Swipe until the soft spots match.

- **Gestures:** → keep (load-bearing) · ← kill (soft/false) · ↑ send to research · long-press = AI’s recommended stance
- **AI:** extracts *assumptions* (not features) + recommended answers
- **Human:** the thumb decides
- **Output:** **Match Sheet** (kept / killed / needs research / forks) + shareable Roast Card
- **Demo line:** *“Tinder for the lies your product tells itself.”*

### 2. DOOMCRACY — coolness 9 · hackathon YES
**Vibe:** TikTok for Socratic punches. Scroll until your idea cries.

- **Gestures:** vertical flick = next question · double-tap = resolved · skip tax = skipped cards return *meaner* · long-press = send punch to a friend
- **Output:** trail of resolved/skipped cards → free PRD
- **Packs:** “YC partner,” “evil VC,” “kind therapist,” “EU compliance gremlin”

### 3. THE INTERN — coolness 9 · hackathon STRETCH
**Vibe:** You swipe. The intern investigates. Loot drops mid-session.

- **Gestures:** ↑ on any card dispatches a research agent; brief slides back into the stack
- **AI:** researches + summarizes with receipts
- **Human:** chooses what to investigate and what findings mean
- **2026 feel:** parallel play — agent while you play

### 4. FORK — coolness 8 · hackathon YES
**Vibe:** Would You Rather, but every fork has blood on it.

- Full-screen A/B dilemmas → after 7 forks, playback of the product you *accidentally* designed
- Share the “You built THIS” poster

### 5. TAROT OF DOUBT — coolness 8 · hackathon YES (demo theater)
**Vibe:** Occult product ritual. Draw three. Face the reading.

- Past (what you’re copying) · Present (hidden assumption) · Future (failure mode)
- 3D flip; tap to challenge the reading → AI revises
- Same brain as CHAR, different costume — killer live demo

### 6. HEATCHECK — coolness 8 · hackathon STRETCH
**Vibe:** BeReal for founders — one daily grill, no polish.

- Random push · 2-min window · voice dump · 3 cards before feed unlocks · blurred roast preview
- Close-friends circle of intellectual honesty

### 7. ROAST RECEIPT — coolness 8 · hackathon YES
**Vibe:** Thermal-printer energy for soft spots.

- Dump → silent grill → scrolling receipt of unpaid assumptions
- Shake for a harsher reprint · iMessage the image

### 8. TWO PHONES — coolness 7 · hackathon YES
**Vibe:** Pass-the-phone co-founder therapy.

- Escalating decks: Flirt → Date → Third Date
- End screen: “compatibility 62% — you disagree on pricing and God”

### 9. LOCKET SPEC — coolness 7 · hackathon STRETCH
**Vibe:** One unresolved question living on your home screen.

- Widget stares at you · 15s voice answer · stamp “resolved”
- Ambient tension as product

### 10. MONUMENT TO A BAD IDEA — coolness 10 · hackathon NO
**Vibe:** Your plan as impossible architecture. Rotate until the crack shows.

- Monument Valley pedagogy for product strategy
- Dream later; too much craft for 3h

## Top pick: CHAR (full sketch)

### First 60 seconds
1. Splash — *Swipe your soft spots.*
2. Pitch dump (text/mic) — **Heat it.**
3. Match-strike theater — *Finding the soft spots…*
4. First assumption card — swiping before the minute ends

### Session
Pitch → AI extracts 12–20 assumptions as a decision tree → swipe stack → every 4th card is a FORK → optional Intern briefs insert → **Match Sheet**

### Match Sheet (the residue)
```
CHAR · Match Sheet
Idea: "…"
Kept: …
Killed: …
Needs research: …
Forks resolved: …
Confidence tax: N unpaid assumptions
```

### Hackathon cut (~3h from Cursor iOS)
**Ship:** pitch → assumption JSON → 3-way swipe → recommended stance → Match Sheet share image  
**Fake proudly:** Intern briefs = one “pretend you searched” call  
**No:** accounts, chat threads, purple gradients

### Costume swap
Same backend, **TAROT OF DOUBT** skin for stage presence if the room wants theater.
